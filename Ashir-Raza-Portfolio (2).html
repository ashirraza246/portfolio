The Challenge

#AS KHUSHBOO was preparing for launch with 6 premium fragrances and 300 bottles of stock — but no system to manage orders, team, inventory, or customer relationships. Everything lived in WhatsApp threads and mental notes. They needed a complete, professional operating system before day one — one that could handle a full team, AI tools, real-time analytics, and a growing customer base.
